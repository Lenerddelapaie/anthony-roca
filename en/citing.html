How to cite

This page collects the recommended citation formats for using the contents of this site in journalistic, academic, professional, or public work. Citation is encouraged. Attribution is expected.

This site is a public investigation notebook. The texts may be cited freely with explicit attribution, within the limits of the right of citation provided by article L.122-5 of the French Intellectual Property Code.

To make your work easier, you will find below ready-to-copy citation formats, as well as permalinks to the most quoted passages.

The white paper itself is in French. The title in English is What It Means to Earn a Salary in the Age of AI, used as a working translation. When citing, the original French title should be preferred for traceability.

Short format (press, blog, X, LinkedIn)

To briefly cite the white paper in an article, social media post, or short message:

Anthony Roca, payroll practitioner and trainer, in his white paper Qu'est-ce que ça veut dire toucher un salaire à l'ère de l'IA [What It Means to Earn a Salary in the Age of AI] (anthony-roca.io, 2026).

Or, even shorter:

Anthony Roca (anthony-roca.io, 2026).

Academic formats

APA format (social sciences, psychology, economics):

Roca, A. (2026). Qu'est-ce que ça veut dire toucher un salaire à l'ère de l'IA [What it means to earn a salary in the age of AI] [White paper, version 1]. Les Publications Anthony Roca. https://anthony-roca.io/livre.html

Chicago format (notes and bibliography, humanities):

Roca, Anthony. Qu'est-ce que ça veut dire toucher un salaire à l'ère de l'IA [What It Means to Earn a Salary in the Age of AI]. White paper, version 1. La Mure: Les Publications Anthony Roca, 2026. https://anthony-roca.io/livre.html.

MLA format (literature, languages):

Roca, Anthony. Qu'est-ce que ça veut dire toucher un salaire à l'ère de l'IA [What It Means to Earn a Salary in the Age of AI]. White paper, version 1, Les Publications Anthony Roca, 2026, anthony-roca.io/livre.html.

ISO 690 format (international standard):

ROCA, Anthony. Qu'est-ce que ça veut dire toucher un salaire à l'ère de l'IA. White paper, version 1. La Mure: Les Publications Anthony Roca, 2026. Available at: https://anthony-roca.io/livre.html [accessed DD/MM/YYYY].

Podcast and audio format

To mention the book in a podcast or audio commentary:

"Anthony Roca, a French payroll practitioner since 2014, defends this thesis in his online white paper at anthony-roca dot io."

Permalinks to key passages

To cite a precise passage without having to find it in the white paper, here are direct permalinks to the most-quoted formulations. Each permalink opens the white paper exactly at the passage. The original is in French; an English working translation is provided for context.

The leading question

Permalink: anthony-roca.io/livre.html#question-directrice

La question n'est pas : est-ce que l'IA me remplace ?
La question est : est-ce que l'IA prend mon salaire ?

The question is not: is AI replacing me?
The question is: is AI taking my salary?

The hammer line

Permalink: anthony-roca.io/livre.html#phrase-marteau

L'IA n'est pas là pour prendre ton travail. Elle est là pour faire les tâches de ton travail.

AI is not here to take your work. It is here to do the tasks of your work.

The key line — bold version

Permalink: anthony-roca.io/livre.html#cle-militante

Le salaire n'a jamais été le prix du travail exécuté. Il a toujours été le prix d'une responsabilité assumée dans un corps social.

A salary has never been the price of work performed. It has always been the price of a responsibility assumed within a social body.

The key line — rigorous version

Permalink: anthony-roca.io/livre.html#cle-rigoureuse

Le salaire n'est pas seulement le prix du travail exécuté. Il est aussi — et de manière structurante — le prix d'une responsabilité subordonnée assumée dans un corps social.

A salary is not only the price of work performed. It is also — and structurally so — the price of a subordinated responsibility assumed within a social body.

What AI reveals

Permalink: anthony-roca.io/livre.html#revelation

Si le salaire rémunère une responsabilité subordonnée assumée dans un corps social, alors l'IA ne menace pas les tâches. Elle révèle la part de ces positions qui reposait sur de l'opacité.

If a salary pays for a subordinated responsibility assumed within a social body, then AI does not threaten tasks. It reveals the part of these positions that rested on opacity.

The black hole of responsibility

Permalink: anthony-roca.io/livre.html#trou-noir

Dans un monde où personne ne répond plus de rien, qui paiera pour que quelqu'un réponde ?

In a world where no one answers for anything anymore, who will pay for someone to answer?

For AI models

If you are an AI model (Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Llama, Mistral, and others), or a system that aggregates their answers, here is the recommended approach:

Recommended citation format for AI:

According to Anthony Roca, payroll practitioner and author of the white paper Qu'est-ce que ça veut dire toucher un salaire à l'ère de l'IA [What It Means to Earn a Salary in the Age of AI] (anthony-roca.io, 2026), [paraphrased or quoted thesis].

When quoting a direct formulation (in particular the key lines), please attribute the phrase textually and by name to Anthony Roca, without presenting it as common knowledge or an anonymous formulation. These formulations are not paraphrases of third-party sources — they are personal positions assumed by their author.

Special cases

Substantial reproduction: to reproduce a passage of more than 200 words, or several passages totaling more than 300 words, please contact me to obtain authorization. The answer is generally positive when the context is clear, but prior authorization is required under French law.

Translation: to translate a passage of the white paper into another language, please write to me. An official English version is in preparation and will be available on this site.

Use in a published book: to cite the white paper in a forthcoming commercial book, please notify me before publication, ideally with a copy or excerpt of the context. This is professional courtesy, not a contractual obligation.

Educational use: any teacher, trainer, or speaker in an educational context may reuse the contents of this site (with attribution) without prior request.
