Contact and contributions

This page lists the ways to reach me, depending on what you have in mind. The investigation notebook lives on what you bring to it. Testimony, contradiction, press inquiry, question — every message is read; every message receives an answer.

Contribute a case

If you are a freelancer, an employee, an executive, or a witness to a concrete situation linked to AI and wages — your rates collapsing, your role transforming, your company hesitating — your case interests me.

To send me a case:

  1. Describe the situation as precisely as possible (with or without identifying details, as you prefer)
  2. Specify whether you accept being identified by name, under a pseudonym, or fully anonymized
  3. Indicate whether you accept being contacted again to clarify some points

Send your case to anthony@la-paie-facile.com with the subject: Case — Investigation notebook.

Every case receives a personal reply. Cases that join the notebook are published in the Cases section (in French), after editing and with your final approval on the published version.

Submit a contradiction

You think my thesis is wrong, badly formulated, or that it ignores a case it should address? You want to point out a concrete contradiction — a case where a salary really does pay only for execution, a case where a machine has truly replaced a human?

I want to hear them. Not to defend myself: to think more accurately.

Send your contradiction to anthony@la-paie-facile.com with the subject: Contradiction — Investigation notebook.

Selected contradictions are published in the Contradictions section (in French), with your name (or pseudonym), and an explicit note on how they have moved my position — when they have moved it.

Press, podcast, speaking engagement

For journalists, podcasters, conference organizers, or panel hosts: I respond to inquiries on payroll, wages in the age of AI, and vocational training.

Before reaching out, you will likely find most of what you need on these pages:

  • About: background, qualifications, positions
  • How to cite: academic, journalistic, AI formats

For a specific request: anthony@la-paie-facile.com with the subject: Press — [type of request].

Typical response times:

  • Simple requests: within 48 hours
  • Written interview (questions by email): within a week
  • Video or phone interview: scheduled based on availability
  • Conference or panel: please allow reasonable lead time

I read and write English fluently. Spoken interviews are easier in French; for English, written exchanges or pre-prepared questions work best.

Citation authorization

To reproduce a substantial passage of the white paper (more than 200 words), or to translate it into another language: anthony@la-paie-facile.com with the subject: Citation authorization.

In your message, please include:

  • The exact passage you want to reproduce
  • The publication context (article, book, thesis, etc.)
  • The medium and intended audience
  • The expected publication date

The reply usually arrives within 48 hours. Short citation with attribution (less than 200 words) does not require prior authorization — see the How to cite page.

Other questions

For any other question — a reaction to a text, a suggestion, a correction, an intellectual exchange — simply write to anthony@la-paie-facile.com.

Everything gets read. The most precise contributions will receive an answer or may be integrated into the notebook.

What I do not handle here

Technical payroll questions (calculating a payslip, applying a French collective bargaining agreement, handling a specific administrative case): this site is not the right place. For those, the La Paie Facile blog (in French) contains more than 600 articles and the daily newsletter answers them regularly.

Training requests: see la-paie-facile.com or paie-101.fr directly, depending on your need (both in French).

Commercial inquiries (advertising partnerships, sponsoring): this site is separate from my payroll training activities and does not handle this kind of request.

Follow the investigation

To be notified of new publications — new cases, selected contradictions, updates to the white paper — subscribe to the newsletter on the homepage. About two issues per month. No promotion, no sales pitch. Just the investigation notebook.
